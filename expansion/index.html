Frequently asked questions

Is this retreat right for where I'm at in my business?

This retreat is designed for entrepreneurs who already have traction and feel a clear pull toward their next level. You don't need to hit a specific revenue number. What matters is that you're actively building and noticing internal friction or capacity limits as you grow.

Will this translate into real business change, or is it mostly inner work?

It translates directly into business change, because the work removes what's been making growth feel heavy.

Day one clears the internal friction that shows up as overthinking, pushing, second-guessing, or plateaued revenue.

Day two turns that clarity into tangible outcomes: cleaner offers, simpler marketing focus, more confident decision-making, and a strategy you can actually follow.

You won't leave inspired and unsure. You'll leave knowing what to do next, and with the internal capacity to do it.

What if I'm not comfortable being vulnerable in a group?

Participation is invitational, and your capacity will be honored throughout the experience. You'll be supported to engage at a pace that feels safe for your nervous system while still allowing meaningful movement.

How hands-on will Jason be during the retreat?

Very. This is a small, intimate experience. Jason will be actively facilitating, teaching, and supporting you throughout both days.

What happens after I apply?

You'll be guided through a short, low-pressure application process. If it's aligned, you'll receive next steps and full event details. If it's not, you'll be told honestly.
